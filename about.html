About Us

Learn more about UMP SafeSpace, our mission, and how we support those affected by gender-based violence and harassment.

Welcome to UMP SafeSpace

Welcome to SafeSpace, a safe and supportive space where you can share your story, connect with others, and find comfort in knowing you're not alone. We're here to listen, to support, and to empower you to heal and grow. Our community is built on empathy, understanding, and kindness, and we invite you to join us with open hearts and minds. Take a deep breath, be yourself, and know that you're among friends who care.

Mission Statement

To provide a safe and non-judgmental space for you to share your story, connect with others, and access resources and support. We provide support and assistance to students who have experienced or witnessed gender-based violence (GBV) or harassment. Our website provides a safe and confidential space for students.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to create a safer and more supportive environment for university students by launching UMP SafeSpace, a web platform that addresses gender-based violence and harassment. We aim to empower students with the resources, support, and community they need to navigate these challenges effectively, fostering a culture of safety, awareness, and resilience at the University of Mpumalanga.
