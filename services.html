Our Services

Discover the range of services we offer to support individuals affected by gender-based violence and harassment.

We're here to help you heal, grow, and thrive. Our services are designed to provide a safe and supportive space for students who have experienced harassment.
We offer confidential counseling, support groups, and resources to help you navigate your journey.


Please don't hesitate to reach out – we're here to support you every step of the way


One-on-One Counseling


Our experienced counselors provide a safe and confidential space for students to share their experiences and receive support.
We offer individual counseling sessions to help students process
their emotions, develop coping strategies, and work towards healing.

- Confidential and non-judgmental support
- Experienced counselors trained in trauma-informed care
- Flexible scheduling to accommodate students' needs
- Goal-oriented support to empower students

Supportive Voices


Connect with others who understand what you're going through.
Our support groups provide a safe space for students to share their experiences, receive support, and connect with peers who have faced similar situations.

- Guided by trained facilitators
- Confidential and non-judgmental environment
- Opportunities to share your story and connect with others
- Supportive community to help you heal and grow
